FEMALE SEXUAL FUNCTION INDEX (FSFI) 女性性功能指數 (FSFI) 評估量表

Confidential Patient Diagnostic Intake 機密病患診斷問卷
Module 1: Desire & Interest 第一部分：性慾望與性興趣

1. Over the past 4 weeks, how often did you feel sexual desire or interest? 1. 過去四週內，您有多常感到性慾望或性興趣？

2. Over the past 4 weeks, how would you rate your level of sexual desire or interest? 2. 過去四週內，您如何評估您的性慾望或性興趣程度？

Module 2: Arousal 第二部分：性喚起（性興奮）

3. Over the past 4 weeks, how often did you feel sexually aroused during sexual activity? 3. 過去四週內，在性活動中您有多常感到性喚起（性興奮）？

4. Over the past 4 weeks, how would you rate your level of sexual arousal during sexual activity? 4. 過去四週內，您如何評估您在性活動中的性喚起程度？

5. Over the past 4 weeks, how confident were you about becoming sexually aroused? 5. 過去四週內，您對於能夠產生性喚起有多大的自信？

6. Over the past 4 weeks, how often have you been satisfied with your arousal? 6. 過去四週內，您有多常對自己的性喚起感到滿意？

Module 3: Lubrication 第三部分：陰道濕潤

7. Over the past 4 weeks, how often did you become lubricated ("wet") during sexual activity? 7. 過去四週內，在性活動中您有多常產生陰道濕潤？

8. Over the past 4 weeks, how difficult was it to become lubricated? 8. 過去四週內，產生陰道濕潤有多困難？

9. Over the past 4 weeks, how often did you maintain your lubrication until completion? 9. 過去四週內，您有多常能維持陰道濕潤直到性活動結束？

10. Over the past 4 weeks, how difficult was it to maintain your lubrication? 10. 過去四週內，維持陰道濕潤有多困難？

Module 4: Orgasm 第四部分：性高潮

11. Over the past 4 weeks, how often did you reach orgasm? 11. 過去四週內，在性刺激或性交時，您有多常達到性高潮？

12. Over the past 4 weeks, how difficult was it for you to reach orgasm? 12. 過去四週內，在性刺激或性交時，達到性高潮有多困難？

13. Over the past 4 weeks, how satisfied were you with your ability to reach orgasm? 13. 過去四週內，您對自己達到性高潮的能力有多滿意？

Module 5: Satisfaction 第五部分：性滿意度

14. Over the past 4 weeks, how satisfied have you been with your emotional closeness? 14. 過去四週內，您對與伴侶在性活動中的情感親密程度有多滿意？

15. Over the past 4 weeks, how satisfied have you been with your sexual relationship? 15. 過去四週內，您對與伴侶的性關係有多滿意？

16. Over the past 4 weeks, how satisfied have you been with your overall sexual life? 16. 過去四週內，您對整體的性生活有多滿意？

Module 6: Pain & Discomfort 第六部分：疼痛與不適

17. Over the past 4 weeks, how often did you experience pain during vaginal penetration? 17. 過去四週內，您在陰道插入時有多常感到疼痛或不適？

18. Over the past 4 weeks, how often did you experience pain following vaginal penetration? 18. 過去四週內，您在陰道插入後有多常感到疼痛或不適？

19. Over the past 4 weeks, how would you rate your level of pain? 19. 過去四週內，您如何評估在陰道插入時或插入後的疼痛或不適程度？
